About Raquel Rivera

Raquel Rivera Virtual Companionship Services was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to combat loneliness and provide authentic human connection to people in New York City and beyond. With years of experience in providing compassionate listening and genuine companionship, Raquel has dedicated herself to creating meaningful conversations that truly matter. Her warm Latina voice and understanding nature have made her a trusted companion for countless individuals seeking real human connection in an increasingly digital world.

At the heart of our service lies a commitment to providing judgment-free, authentic companionship available 24/7. We believe that everyone deserves to be heard, valued, and understood. Our bilingual approach ensures that language is never a barrier to connection. Whether you're looking for late-night conversations, emotional support, or simply someone who genuinely cares about what you have to say, Raquel is here to provide the warm, human connection that makes a real difference in your daily life. We're dedicated to creating a safe, confidential space where you can be completely yourself.
